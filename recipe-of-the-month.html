Recipe of the Month

Chicken Adobo


Ingredients


  • 500g Chicken
  • 1 onion
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp peppercorns
  • 3 laurel leaves
  • 2 cups water

Instructions


  1. Heat the pan and oil, then lightly fry the onion and garlic until golden brown.
  2. Add the chicken and saut√® until fork tender.
  3. In a bowl mix the soy sauce, peppercorns and water
  4. Add the mixture and the laurel leaves to the pan and let it simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Add vinegar. Stir and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Put-in the sugar and salt. Stir and turn the heat off. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
